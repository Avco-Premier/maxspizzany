Our Story

Welcome to
Max's Pizzeria

Since 1999, Max's Pizzeria has been proudly serving the Inwood community with authentic Italian flavors and genuine family hospitality. Every pizza is crafted with care using time-honored techniques passed down through generations.

We're committed to making every meal memorable, whether you're craving a classic margherita or exploring our delicious vegetarian and vegan options. Our family recipes have stood the test of time, bringing the authentic taste of Italy to Long Island.

Located in the heart of Inwood, we're easily accessible and offer convenient parking. Our welcoming atmosphere makes it perfect for family dinners, quick lunches, or picking up your online order.
